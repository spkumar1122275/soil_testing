What should I do next?
Stop drinking or using the water immediately. Next investigate and find the source of the contamination. Take any necessary steps to disinfect the water. Conduct further tests to ensure the water is clear of all contamination. Check our website for more information.
Information
The presence of coliform bacteria in drinking water usually indicates the possibility of contamination by disease-causing organisms (pathogens).
