What should I do next?
Continue ongoing monitoring of the water quality. The water quality should be checked at least every six months. Track any observable change in the water such as increased turbidity, staining, or hardness, or a change in taste or odor that could indicate contamination.
Information
The presence of coliform bacteria in drinking water usually indicates the possibility of contamination by disease-causing organisms (pathogens).
